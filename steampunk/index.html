Steampunk

What is steampunk?

Let’s try Google to help out on this one:

 

“Steampunk refers to a subgenre of science fiction and sometimes fantasy—also in recent years a fashion and lifestyle movement—that incorporates technology and aesthetic designs inspired by 19th-century industrial steam-powered machinery.”

 

So why are we using the steampunk theme for our wedding? Because it will be fun! It encapsulates the fantasy and excitement for a new adventure in life. And because we can’t say “no” to a party without dress-up.

That sounds awesome! But how do I get involved?

Everyone will have to be dressed in steampunk styled clothing on the day. But don’t worry, it’s not that difficult!

 

The first part is finding clothes that match the Victorian era. For the ladies or gentlemen, you can dress in formal or informal wear (the point is to look awesome and have fun!).

 

For those who are Facebook savvy and in the Brisbane area, you can find some great costumes at UrsaLady Creations.

 

If you’re near Brisbane, we recommend Cassandra’s Closet. If not, your local costume or dance shop should have something that can help you look the part. You can also try the online store Costume Box for costumes and accessories.

 

It’s important to accessorise your Victorian wear. This is the fun part! You can use the links above for Custome Box, or use Pinterest or Google for inspiration. Click the button below to see a list of steampunk ideas Chantal has created.
